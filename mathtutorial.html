Quick Maths.

The console is capable of doing maths on the fly using the +, -, *, and / functions.

Such functions can help in on the fly calculations just like a normal calculator. Despite this, this technique is outdated and is not useful for debugging anyways. To actually debug, you need to know what .Math functions are. .Math Functions can be use to set variables and deal with complex numerical operations such as raising powers or finding minimums and maximums from a set of numbers. Eg: 10+5; 10/2;

Math. Functions.

Here are some Math. functions:

Powers

Math.pow(X, Y) will raise a X to the power of Y. Eg: var foo = Math.pow(2, 2);
console.log(foo); 4

Square Root

Math.sqrt(X) will give you the square root of the given value X. Eg: var foo = Math.sqrt(4);
console.log(foo); 2

Min/Max

Math.min/max(X, Y, Z) will give you minimum or maximum of a range. Eg: var foo = Math.min(2, 3, 4);
console.log(foo); 2
